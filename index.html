1. How does the app integrate with WhatsApp?

Our app seamlessly detects shopping-related messages from WhatsApp and automatically converts them into an organized shopping list.

2. Do I need to manually add items, or does the app recognize them automatically?

You have the flexibility to either add items manually or allow the app to identify and extract shopping items directly from your messages.

3. Can I share my shopping list with others?

Yes, the app supports real-time list synchronization, enabling seamless sharing with family members, roommates, or anyone in your household.

4. Does the app function offline?

Yes, you can access, view, and update your shopping list even without an internet connection, ensuring uninterrupted usability.

5. How is my data protected?

We prioritize user privacy and security. All data is securely encrypted, and we do not share or sell any user information.

6. How can I get support if I encounter an issue?

For any assistance, please visit our support page or contact us via email. Our team is available to help and typically responds within two hours.
